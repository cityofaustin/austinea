Use Case Scope

Evaluate the current salary ranges for classifications to ensure they are competitive. Classifications include all the attributes of a position and a job. Title, pay grade, scope, responsibilities, exemption status. A group of positions sufficiently similar in duties and responsibilities such that the same title may be used to designate each of the positions.
