Obtain Municipal Court Search and Seizure Warrant
Use Case Model

Scope: A CodeCase (typically a CodeViolationCase) requires a SearchWarrant to proceed. An Austin Code Officer seeks City Legal Approval for a SearchWarrant. An Affidavit and SearchWarrant are produced by the Austin Code Officer, and then approved by the City Legal Department. The SearchWarrant is approved by a Court Judge and then SearchWarrant is served. If the SearchWarrant is not served within 3 calendar days of approval, the SearchWarrant must be approved again per the Serve Warrant use case. If the SearchWarrant or Affidavit require changes the Obtain City Legal Approval use case is restarted. If the SearchWarrant is successfully served the Austin Code Officer performs and Inspection, creates an InventoryList of their Inspection, and then updates their CodeViolationCase with the information.
