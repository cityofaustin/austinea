Provide Assistance Services
Use Case Model

Scope: If a need is identified by the Austin Code Officer, the Public Information Office Admin assesses the Citizen's needs per the AssistanceResult (special needs, housing, food, mattresses and transportation). The Public Information Office Admin creates a ServiceCase and updates the CodeViolationCase with notes from the ServiceCase. The Public Information Office Admin contacts Health and Human Services for a service consultation. Multiple departments are involved in the service consultation: Health and Human Services, Red Cross, HSEM, Capitol Metro and Capitol Food Bank. The Public Information Office Admin creates the HHSDAssessmentForm. The Public Information Office Admin requests the Chain of Command approve the HHSDAssessmentForm. The Chain of Command either approves the HHSDAssessmentForm or requests the Public Information Office Admin recreate the HHSDAssessmentForm. The Public Information Office Admin requests External Resources provide the services identified previously (ServiceCaseActionItem). Weekly reviews are conducted by the Public Information Office Admin for the RelocationCase with Chain of Command (PIO Supervisor, HHSD, etc.) Review includes food needs, special needs, etc. If new services are required, the Public Information Office requests External Resources provide the services requested. Reviews continue until the RelocationCase is closed. When services are no longer required, the Public Information Office Admin closes the ServiceCase. The Public Information Office Admin updates the CodeViolationCase with notes on the ServiceCase.

Provide Assistance Services
Sequence Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Public Information Office Admin/assessCitizenServiceNeeds
Based on the AssistanceResult, the Public Information Office Admin assesses the Citizen's needs (CitizenNeed) including IncomeInformation, NumberOfOccupants, Age, School, IsEmployeed, Location, Barriers (criminal history) and MedicalHistory. Public Information Office Admin uses TCAD, Austin Energy, WCAD, HCAD, CC&B, AWU and APD to research Citizen's information and identifies ServiceAssessment. Alternatively, the Public Information Office Admin begins the assessment using a CommunityOutreachForm.
002 Public Information Office Admin/createServiceCase
The Public Information Office Admin creates a ServiceCase (also known as a relocation case) email, which includes all assessment information from assessCitizenServiceNeeds, and sends to External Resources (other City departments).
003 Case Management System/updateCodeViolationCase
The user updates the CodeViolationCase with relevant information to the case. If the CodeViolationCase generated a StopWorkOrder, the StopWorkOrder is attached to the CodeViolationCase. The user attaches a copy of the DiagramOfWorkRequired. If a board and secure occurs, a BoardAndSecureBill is attached to the CodeViolationCase by Austin Code Admin. If a sign was removed, the Austin Code Officer adds the sign and information to the CodeViolationCase. Additionally, if CautionBarriers were placed, information on the CautionBarriers is added to the CodeViolationCase. If property information was updated, the Case Management System includes updated property information. The user updates the CodeViolationCase with information relevant to the change. This includes, but is not limited to, BoardAndSecureBill, PropertyAbatementBill, FollowUpNotes, photographs (boarded, non-boarded, posted notifications, placards), emails, PhoneCallSummary, other relevant information and inter-department communications. If a ServiceCase is created, the Public Information Office Admin adds notes to the CodeViolationCase including the CitizenNeed and ServicesRecommended for ServiceCase. If CorrectiveResources are identified, the user updates the CodeViolationCase with a list of CorrectiveResources provided (three resources) and notes of contact with Owner/Tenant. HardshipReason is added to the CodeViolationCase. If new violations are identified, the user adds the new violation to the Case Management System and the violation is attached to the original CodeComplaintCase. If a TitleSearch is completed, the TitleSearch is added to the CodeViolationCase. If additional LegallyInterestedParties are identified, the parties are added to the CodeViolationCase. If a NoticeOfViolation is generated, the NoticeOfViolation is added to the CodeViolationCase. If posted at the property, photographs are attached to the CodeViolationCase. If the NoticeOfViolation is delivered and a certified mail receipt is received, the receipt is added to the CodeViolationCase. If a citation is created, the Case Management System attaches the citation to the CodeViolationCase. Additionally, the user scans and uploads a copy of the physical citation to the Case Management System. If an AdministrativeHearingWarning is issued, the user updates the CodeViolationCase with information on the issued AdministrativeHearingWarning. If an AdministrativeHearingCitation is issued, the user updates the CodeViolationCase with the AdministrativeHearingCitation, and a LegalCase is created. The user scans and uploads returned liens to the CodeViolationCase. If a lien is filed for the property, the TRVNumber is added to the CodeViolationCase.
004 Health and Human Services/provideServiceConsultation
The Public Information Office Admin contacts Health and Human Services for a consultation of the previously sent ServiceCase. Health and Human Services provides a DepartmentContactList and identifies ActionItems for various departments.
005 Public Information Office Admin/createHHSDAssessmentForm
The Public Information Office Admin creates an HHSDAssessmentForm with the assistance of the Citizen (either by phone or in person). The HHSDAssessmentForm includes Name, Date, Income, Address, CriminalHistory, MedicalHistory, Employment, ServicesReceived, Household (family) and School. The HHSDAssessmentForm includes the ServiceCaseActionItem list.
006 Chain of Command/approveHHSDAssessmentForm
The Public Information Office Admin contacts the Chain of Command (by email or phone) for approval of the HHSDAssessmentForm (service plan). The Chain of Command provides notes on revisions needed, if any.
007 External Resources/provideServices
The Public Information Office Admin contacts External Resources (by phone or email) to provide Services per the HHSDAssessmentForm (service plan). External Resources provide information back to the Public Information Office Admin on whether Citizen qualifies for Services and actions they can take.
008 Public Information Office Admin/assessServiceCase
The Public Information Office Admin assesses the ServiceCase including contacting External Resources for information on Services rendered. The Public Information Office Admin contacts the Citizen to determine if they have additional ServiceNeeds or if they no longer require Services.
009 Chain of Command/reviewServiceCase
The Public Information Office Admin requests the Chain of Command to review the ServiceCase to determine if the ServiceCase may be closed. The meeting includes ServiceCaseActionItem list, new Services and the Status of Services the Citizen is receiving.
010 Public Information Office Admin/closeServiceCase
The Public Information Office Admin notifies External Resources by email that the ServiceCase is closed.
