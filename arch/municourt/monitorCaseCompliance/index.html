Monitor Case Compliance
Use Case Model

Scope: The Defendant either complies or fails to comply with appearances, payments and sentencing guidelines mandated the law on their case. The case management system monitors the Defendant's compliance by keeping track of appearance dates, payments and the Defendant's timely filing proof of sentence completion.

Monitor Case Compliance
Sequence Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Defendant/complyWithCaseConditions
The Defendant has the responsibility to manage any deadlines, appearances, payments, sentencing programs or DSC completion and additional requirements on their case. The Defendant either stays within compliance by paying, appearing and submitting proof to the Court in a timely manner or they don't. Every time the defendant's compliance is evaluated a compliance result value is generated and the case management system will take the appropriate action based upon that result (including no action).
002 Manage Court Case/retrieveCaseFileByCaseNumber
The case management system retrieves the case file using the provided case number.
003 Manage Court Case/monitorAppearance
If appearance events are scheduled the case management system checks for any upcoming appearance dates (phone dialer notification) or failure to appear conditions on the case. If the defendant is out of compliance then a compliance action is generated.
004 Manage Court Case/monitorCaseBalance
If there is a balance on the case greater than $25 the case management system monitors the case balance including the last payment date, the next payment plan due date until the case is paid off or dismissed. If the defendant is out of compliance (past due payment) then a compliance action is generated.
005 Manage Court Case/monitorCommunityService
If there is a sentence to community service the case management system monitors any community service on the case until it is completed and all compliance paperwork is submitted. If the defendant is out of compliance (no proof of service complying with the individual's community service schedule on record) then a compliance action is generated.
006 Manage Court Case/monitorWarrantStatus
If there is an outstanding warrant on the defendant's case the case management system monitors the warrant status on the case. If the defendant is out of compliance (there is an active warrant on the case) then a compliance action is generated.
