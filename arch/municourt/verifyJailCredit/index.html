Verify Jail Credit
Use Case Model

Scope: The Defendant requests Jail Credit Verification and presents a photo ID to the Court Clerk.
 
If the Defendant has a valid ID:

  • The Court Clerk verifies the person's identity in the case management system and retrieves the PID associated with the Defendant.
  • The Court Clerk runs the jail credit query in Tiburon using the Defendants's name, date of birth, and time frame of incarceration to retrieve the Jail Credit information and confirm that there is Jail Credit.

 
If the Defendant does not have a valid ID to use for confirmation of their identity then the Court Clerk directs the Defendant to the Walk-In Court to determine if the Judge will grant an exception. The Clerk may generate the Tiburon Jail Credit Report for the Defendant using provided demographics and walk it over to the court and present it to the Judge (but not delivering it to the Defendant).

Verify Jail Credit
Sequence Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Manage Court Case/generateJailCreditVerificationForm
If the Defendant is not in custody:
 
The Court Clerk, Customer (Defendant), or Jail Custodian requests the Jail Credit Verification form. The case management system generates the form and delivers it to the requestor either as
  • (If the Defendant is not a walk-in) a web form to be completed and submitted electronically
  • (If the Defendant is not a walk-in) a form (file) attached to an email or text message to be printed, completed and returned by mail or as a scanned file via email or text message
  • (If the Defendant is not a walk-in) a paper form delivered by the mail to be completed and returned by mail or as a scanned file via email or text message
  • (If the Defendant is a walk-in) a paper form delivered to the Defendant (or requestor) at the Court lobby front counter to be completed and returned to the Court for processing
002 Manage Court Case/completeJailCreditForm
The Defendant completes the Jail Credit Verification form and submits it to the Court either
  • via the web
  • electronically as a scanned file (from a printed copy of the form) attached to an email or text message
  • a paper form delivered by the mail to the Court's mail address
003 Defendant/requestJailCredit
The incarcerated Defendant requests a Jail Credit form from the Court (Collections or Communications units). The request is submitted to the Court by either the Defendant's representative or by the Jail Custodian.
004 Jail Custodian/completeJailCreditForms
The Jail Custodian completes the Jail Credit form on behalf of the Defendant in custody. The form and verification of the jail time served at the institution of incarceration outside Travis County is delivered to the Court by mail or as attachments (scanned files) to an email or text message.
005 Court Clerk/reviewJailCreditSubmission
The Defendant submits the completed Jail Credit form as well as proof of incarceration (which could be the assertion that the jail time was served in Travis County and can be verified by a TIBURON inquiry.) The form is verified by the Court Clerk for completeness. If the form is completed correctly it is forwarded along with the proof of incarceration to the Central Booking Clerk.
006 Tiburon/retrieveJailCreditInfo
If the Defendant served jail time in Travis County, the Court Clerk (Central Booking) runs the jail credit query in Tiburon using the Defendants's name, date of birth, and time frame of incarceration to retrieve the Jail Credit information and confirm that there is Jail Credit.
007 Jail Custodian/retrieveJailCreditInfo
If the Defendant served time outside of Travis County, the Central Booking Clerk contacts the institution of incarceration outside Travis County, providing the Defendant's demographics and the dates of incarceration, to verify that the Defendant served the jail time indicated on the submitted proof. The Jail Custodian verifies the information.
008 Judge/putJailCreditRequestInJudgesQueue
If Jail Credit form and proof of incarceration are valid and complete: The Central Booking Clerk puts the request for Jail Credit Verification (including the completed form, proof of incarceration/TIBURON report) in the Judge's queue for review and action.
009 Judge/reviewJailCreditVerification
The Judge reviews the Jail Credit request and either approves or rejects it. The result is a Judge's order specifying a case balance credit amount for the jail time served that is returned to Central Booking for a case update.
010 Manage Court Case/applyJailCredit
If the Jail Credit is approved by the Judge: The Central Booking Clerk receives the judicial order specifying the case balance credit amount to be applied to the case. The Clerk submits the case balance credit amount to the case management system and the credit is applied to the current balance on the case resulting in a new (reduced) balance. An case action is generated to be added to the case.
011 Manage Court Case/addActionToCase
An action is applied to the case: Jail Credit Verification activity the action codes include:
  • JCASORD - Jail Case Order Form
  • JRTN - Jail Case Order Form - Return
  • JTLOF - Jail Case Order Form - Total Layout
  • PROOJ - Proof Submitted - Jail Credit Letter
  • JC - Request for Jail Credit
  • RJCR - Response to Jail Credit Request
