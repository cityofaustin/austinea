Manage Dialer Notices Interaction Overview

Scope: As cases become qualified they are added to dialer campaigns where the defendant is contacted and notified about

  • Initial court appearances
  • Upcoming case events
  • Past due payments
  • Cases transitioning to a warrant status
The Manage Dialer Notices Interaction Action Overview diagram shows use cases in their logical association with each other as part of the overall dialer notification process. The dialer notification process monitors cases as
  • Case compliance is monitored for conditions triggering a dialer notification
  • Cases are uploaded to a notification vendor for a dialer campaign
  • Cases processed in a dialer notification campaign have their results recorded as actions on the case
