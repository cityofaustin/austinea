Manage Collections Interaction Overview

Scope: As cases become qualified they are added to collections. The Collections Interaction Action Overview diagram shows use cases in their logical association with each other as part of the overall collection process. The collection process monitors cases as they

  • Change to and from warrant status
  • Restart from a paused state
  • Pause due to events on the case
  • Qualify for withdrawl from collections
  • Receive payments to the collection service
  • Receive payments at the Court
  • Have payments reversed
  • Have the collection fee modified due to events and conditions on the case
  • Experience a case balance modification
