Manage Court Case is the next capability with the most number of goals.

Based on model analysis, it appears that the goals Deliver effective customer service and Document Municipal Court processes contribute to the largest number of capabilities. This is usually a good starting point. If you observe the goal dependencies, you'll observe that Document Municipal Court processes is a critical goal to achieve other important goals. This is not a surprise because we should next explore the processes relevant to Manage Court Case capability specifically toward Develop future needs requirements for case management improvements. This goal depends on Document Municipal Court processes necessary for future needs requirements. In addition, this goal contributes to other goal dependencies and it is needed by contracting to negotiate the future case management system. Your team might begin thinking about "important outcomes" and their related processes that you wish incorporate as improvements to your current case management system capabilities.
