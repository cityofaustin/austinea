CapabilityDescription
Manage Radio NetworkManage radio network to include voice radio communications and supporting infrastructure used by public safety, other public service agencies (non-enterprise general fund), and some enterprise departments such as utilities. Provide geographic coverage for Travis County and City of Austin. Requires support from other network services.
Provide Broadband Network AccessUsing commercial wireless carrier connections such as Long-Term Evolution (LTE), provide broadband users such as public safety and other public service agencies access to the City network using various controlled access privileges.
Provide WiFi Network AccessProvide reliable WiFi network access to include Internet and City network - includes public and private WiFi Internet access points.
Provide Voice Recording ServicesProvide voice recording services for Public Safety, non-enterprise general fund public service agencies and some enterprise departments.
Equip Public Service and Safety VehiclesInstall vehicle electronic equipment and hardware (ex., two way radios, mobile data computers, digital video system, emergency lighting, weapons security systems, etc.). Maintain vehicle electronic equipment and hand-held devices such as radio, electronic citation writers and printers, radar guns, lasers, biometric devices (finger print readers).
Maintain Flood and Early Warning System Data NetworkMaintain licensed radio wireless data network for the City owned Flood Early Warning System.
