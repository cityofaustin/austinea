Perform Adjustment to Utility Bill
Use Case Model

Scope: The Customer calls into AE's call center, or the ARR Pro center to Inquire about an adjustment. If the original request is made through AE's call center, a "To Do" activity is created and forwarded to the ARR Pro center. The ARR call center CSR. If more information is needed, the ARR Call center will reach out to the customer. If a field check is needed (verification of the cart at the premise) The ARR Call center CSR will initiate the check using CC&B, who then propagates it to the Solid waste Tracking System. The ARR Call Center CSR sends the request to the Utility Account Analyst. The Utility Account analyst will research the "to do" by using Google Maps, using CC&B, or Solid Waste Tracking system, And make a decision about approval or denial of the adjustment to the customers billing. If the request is approved, the Utility Account analyst will create a "to do" in the CC&B billing system, and a contact note is added to the account. The request is forwarded to the AE Billing team for resolution. If issues are discovered, the AE Billing team will send an email requesting additional information to provision the request. The Utility Account Analyst will provide requested information. If the request is denied the Utility Account analyst will add a "contact note" on the original "To Do" within CC&B, and a contact note on the account itself (within CC&B). The original AE CSR will reach out to the customer and indicate actions taken.

*NOTE: The adjustment will be effective within 1 to 2 billing cycles

Req# Type/Interface - Requirement Description Performance
001 ARR Call Center CSR/requestBillModification
The ARR Call center CSR records the concern for the customer. Ensures the customer is within the service area of the Austin Resource Recovery. They look up the ARR record for the customer based on address.
002 CC&B/verifyCustomerInformation
The CSR looks up the customer account using the address. The Customer is asked three "verifying questions" (address, account holder's name, account number) to validate they are the customer at the address in question. The CSR associate will then ask details associated with the problem.

The AE Call center CSR records the concern for the customer. Ensures the customer is within the service area of the Austin Resource Recovery. They look up the ARR record for the customer based on address.
004 CC&B/verifyCustomerInformation
The CSR looks up the customer account using the address. The Customer is asked three "verifying questions" (address, account holder's name, account number) to validate they are the customer at the address in question. The CSR associate will then ask details associated with the problem.
005 CC&B/requestBillModificationFromAE
The AE Utility CSR records the concern for the customer. Ensures the customer is within the service area of the Austin Resource Recovery. They look up the ARR record for the customer based on address.
006 Customer/provideInformation
The ARR Call Center CSR reaches out to the customer to verify or clarify information in question.  
007 CC&B/initiateFieldCheck
The ARR call center CSR uses CC&B to create a field activity. The following information is provided: The Type of Field activity ("FIELD CHECK"), an Instructional note, the date the check is requested.
008 Solid Waste Tracking System/initiateFieldCheck
CC&B communicates with Solid Waste Tracking System the Address, the customer name, the information note, the date requested, the field activity type, the cart type and cart size in question. 
009 CC&B/forwardTODOToUtilityAccountAnalystQueue
The ARR Call Center CSR looks at the billing calendar to determine the proper Utility Account Analyst covering the TODO's for that date. 
010 CC&B/evaluateTODOActivity
The ARR Utility Analyst accesses CC&B and reads the TODO activity on the customer record. They check the billing history, the appropriate fees, Customer contacts, and the cart size for which the customer is being billed.
011 Google Maps/examiningPropertyImages
The Analyst uses Google Maps to examine the images associated with the address. This may include evaluating images from previous years as well. This is all accomplished via the Google Maps service.
012 CC&B/evaluateCustomerAccount
The ARR Utility Analyst accesses CC&B and evaluates the customer record. They check the billing history, the appropriate fees, Customer contacts, and the cart size for which the customer is being billed. 
013 Solid Waste Tracking System/evaluateSolidWastePickupInformation
The Utility Analyst accesses the Solid Waste Tracking System to determine the cart sizes when the customer took possession of the property, if a cart was delivered to the customer at any time, or any other cart related activities at the property.
014 CC&B/modifyAccountToAccommodateFix
The User uses CC&B to correct information associated with the request. This might be carts sizes, or cart usage or other billing concerns (i.e. customer base fee, clean community fee, etc.) This could also be the final adjustment to the bill as well. 
015 CC&B/createToDoAndAddContactNote
The Utility Account Analyst accesses CC&B and creates a new TODO request indicating modification to the utility account is necessary, and adds contact notes to the original TODO record. 
016 AE Billing Team/modifyBill
CC&B routes the TODO request to the AE Billing team. 
017 AE Billing Team/sendEmailToARRRequestingClarification
The AE billing team sends email requesting clarification from the ARR Utility analysts. 
018 AE Billing Team/recordAdditionalInformation
The ARR Utility Analyst provides the requested information.
019 CC&B/modifyAccountToAccommodateFix
The User uses CC&B to correct information associated with the request. This might be carts sizes, or cart usage or other billing concerns (i.e. customer base fee, clean community fee, etc.) This could also be the final adjustment to the bill as well. 
020 CC&B/addContactNote
The ARR Utility Analyst adds a contact note to the original TODO indicating the request is denied. And also adds a contact note on the customer account. This include the reason for denial.
