Manage Kennel Space
Use Case Model

Scope: An animal is identified as needing to be housed in a kennel. This is either through a request from Customer Service or via two reports that are run daily: the Move List, and the Surgery List. If the facility is at capacity, the animal is moved to a temporary kennel, and an attempt is made to place the animal at the Town Lake Animal Center or with a Rescue or Foster Organization. If a kennel is available, and the animal is injured, it will be moved to a non-public medical ward. If a kennel is available, the animal is a stray and has no illness, it's moved to a public kennel. If a kennel is available, the animal is a stray and is unwell, it's moved to a public kennel isolation kennel. If a kennel is available, and the animal has bitten someone, it's moved to a non-public quarantine kennel. If a kennel is available, the animal owner has been identified, the animal is moved to a non-public kennel. Once the animal is placed into a kennel, Animal Care uses the system to generate a kennel card, and update the animal information to indicate the new location of the animal. As a final step, once an animal has been moved from a kennel, Animal Care will clean the vacated kennel location if possible.

Manage Kennel Space
Sequence Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/generateMoveList
Animal Care uses the system to generate a report that indicates all animals scheduled to be moved. The report is segregated by low-, medium-, and high-priority levels, as well as preference to where the animal should be moved. This report shows animal ID, current kennel, to-do date and time, age of animal, sex, weight, description, memo, and when the animal is due out of the kennel (date of medical treatment or bite quarantine).
002 Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/generateSurgeryList
Animal Care uses the system to generate a report for the current day that indicates which animals to be moved from their kennel to surgery.
003 Animal Care/requestAnimalBeRelocated
Customer Service contacts Animal Care via radio, phone or email, and requests an animal be moved to another kennel (or is about to leave the facility).
004 Customer Service/placeAnimalInTemporaryKennel
Customer Service places the animal into a temporary holding kennel (rolling cage, pop-up crate, etc.) until a location can be determined.
005 Animal Care/transportAnimalToTownLake
Animal Care monitors the facility and determines if there is available space at the Town Lake Animal Center (TLAC). They load the animal into a transport vehicle and take it to the TLAC.
006 Rescue Coordinator/requestAssistanceAnimalHousing
Having no available kennel space, Animal Care contacts the Rescue Coordinator requesting the animal be placed with a Rescue Partner.
007 Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/addAndUpdatePersonAndAnimalInfo
Customer Service/Foster Coordinator/Other users will access the system and add or update person and animal info. They will include information like Animal Owner, owner address, pet name, pet breed, pet sex, pet microchip info, rabies certificate information, as well as other important information. If the animal or person is already in the system, Customer Service uses the system to update the information. In the case of Trappers, add the date they signed the Standards of Conduct and the Standard Operating Procedure. In the event of volunteer applicant for Trappers, the Outreach Education Team will access the system and provide information from the TrapperApplication to create a PersonInfo record for the Applicant (including verification date and time for the signing of the Standard of Conduct and the Standard Operating Procedure), if one does not already exist. If a PersonInfo record exists, the Outreach Education Team will update the information as necessary based on the Applicant’s information. For spay/neuter, the user modifies the sex of the animal from M/F (male/female) to S/N (spay/neuter), and records the date of surgery, date processed and vet clinic ID (the system maintains a list of all vet clinics on record within the area). Foster Coordinators/Rescue Coordinators create PersonInfo records for Citizens acting/assisting with foster care, and annotate AnimalInfo records with information indicating Foster Care Providers who have taken ownership of the animals.
008 Foster Coordinator/requestAssistanceAnimalHousing
***BLANK***
009 Animal Care/moveAnimalToNonPublicMedicalWard
Animal Care moves the animal to a non-public medical ward for veterinary services treatment. This is normally in response to a request from veterinary services.
010 Animal Care/moveAnimalToPublicKennel
Animal Care moves the animal to a public kennel. This is normally in response to an animal in holding or a new animal entering the facility.
011 Animal Care/moveAnimalToPublicISOKennel
Animal Care moves the animal to an isolation kennel for veterinary services treatment. This is normally in response to a request from veterinary services.
012 Animal Care/moveAnimalToNonPublicQuarantineKennel
Animal Care moves the animal to a non-public quarantine kennel for veterinary services observation (240 hours). This is per state regulation, and normally related to an animal that bites or has interacted with a potential rabies carrier (e.g., bats, raccoons, foxes, coyotes, skunks).
013 Animal Care/moveAnimalToNonPublicKennel
Animal Care moves the animal to a non-public kennel for housing. This is normally in response to knowing the owner or a potential criminal/legal case in which the animal is involved.
014 Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/generateKennelCard
Animal Care uses the system to generate a kennel card. This card includes a picture of the animal, the animal ID, intake date, outcome date, sex, date animal is available, microchip number, surgery date (if applicable), animal name, color, whether the animal has lived with children or other animals, rabies tag number (if it has one), lab results, staff pick and reason the animal is a staff pick, and public accessibility (is the animal on APD, behavioral, age or medical lock?).
015 Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/updateAnimalInfo
The User uses the system to update information on the AnimalInfo record. This includes specifying memos, and providing delivery information, kennel assignment or changes, and recording any fees incurred with animal delivery and care. The User enters all medical, behavior and preventative care administered to the animal. The system logs the user ID and the date and time of every change or transaction acted upon in the system.
016 Animal Care/cleanKennel
Animal Care cleans the kennel of an animal that has recently been vacated (or moved to surgery). This is normally triggered by a manual process-cards that are flipped from green to red indicating the need for cleaning or clean/dirty cards.
