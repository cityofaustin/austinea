Perform Spay Neuter Followup
Use Case Model

Scope: Animal is adopted via the "Manage Pet Adoption" process, during which it is established if the animal needs sterilization. The Adoptee signs a Spay and Neuter Agreement when taking possession of the animal. The Admin Personnel uses the system to record this commitment. Weekly, Admin Personnel uses the system to generate reminder cards to be sent to Adoptees, and uses the system to generate a "weekly call list." The Admin Personnel attempts to contact each delinquent Adoptee, requesting proof of sterilization be provided. Based on the information/animal disposition communicated, the Admin Personnel will contact the Vet referenced to confirm the surgery. Monthly, the Admin Personnel uses the system to generate a report of all people who are delinquent by 30 days or more. The Admin Personnel constructs a certified letter to each delinquent Adoptee providing notification of the issuance of a citation after 10 days. The Admin Personnel invokes the Issue Citation process for all non-compliant Adoptees 10 business days after the certified letter has been sent. If the Adoptee provides verifiable confirmation of the animal being sterilized, it is recorded within the system, and the Manage Deposits process is used to either provide refund of deposits made during adoption or change the deposit to a donation. During this process, the Admin Personnel will record all attempts to contact Adoptee and all their conversations within the system.

Perform Spay Neuter Followup
Sequence Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Admin Personnel/recordSpayAndNeuterAgreement
Customer Service will make copies of the Spay and Neuter Agreement, and then deliver the original to Admin Personnel, who file the document for later retrieval (should legal action be required).
002 Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/addAndUpdatePersonAndAnimalInfo
Customer Service/Foster Coordinator/Other users will access the system and add or update person and animal info. They will include information like Animal Owner, owner address, pet name, pet breed, pet sex, pet microchip info, rabies certificate information, as well as other important information. If the animal or person is already in the system, Customer Service uses the system to update the information. In the case of Trappers, add the date they signed the Standards of Conduct and the Standard Operating Procedure. In the event of volunteer applicant for Trappers, the Outreach Education Team will access the system and provide information from the TrapperApplication to create a PersonInfo record for the Applicant (including verification date and time for the signing of the Standard of Conduct and the Standard Operating Procedure), if one does not already exist. If a PersonInfo record exists, the Outreach Education Team will update the information as necessary based on the Applicant's information. For spay/neuter, the user modifies the sex of the animal from M/F (male/female) to S/N (spay/neuter), and records the date of surgery, date processed and vet clinic ID (the system maintains a list of all vet clinics on record within the area). Foster Coordinators/Rescue Coordinators create PersonInfo records for Citizens acting/assisting with foster care, and annotate AnimalInfo records with information indicating Foster Care Providers who have taken ownership of the animals.
003 Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/generateReminderCards
Admin Personnel uses the system to generate a report for manually creating "reminder cards"-these are postcards that include the Animal ID, date by which the animal is to be sterilized, a contact phone number, a fax number, and address of the animal shelter. Additionally, Admin Personnel uses a Dymo label machine to print mailing labels for the postcards.
004 Admin Personnel/recordProofOfSpayNeuter
The Adoptee sends the sterilization information, which includes specific vet clinic that performed the surgery and the date on which the surgery occurred, as well as the client's name.
005 Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/generateWeeklyCallList
Admin Personnel uses the system to generate a report of all animals adopted (currently dubbed the "weekly call report"). It provides, by date range, a list of all the animals past due for spay/neuter by 10-14 calendar days. The report provides Adoptee, phone number, animal ID and animal name.
006 Admin Personnel/contactAdopteeAboutSpayNeuterAgreement
Admin Personnel calls or emails the Adoptee (if voicemail is reached, a message is left and an email is sent if there's an address on file). The message includes the animal ID, date adopted, COA phone and fax number, and the date by which the animal was supposed to be sterilized.
007 Adoptee/provideSpayOrNeuterInfo
Admin Personnel requests the proof of sterilization. At this time, Admin Personnel asks what the animal is now named.
008 Admin Personnel/sendCustomerCopyOfSpayNeuterAgreement
Admin Personnel scans the original signed Spay/Neuter Agreement, and indicates that the deadline for sterilization is extended. The extension varies based on the circumstances for noncompliance.
009 Admin Personnel/contactVetToVerifyPetTreatment
Admin Personnel contacts the Vet and verifies the date that the animal was sterilized.
010 Provide Animal Rescue and Shelther/generate30DateDelinquentLIst
Admin Personnel uses the system to generate a 30-day delinquent list. They provide a 30-day date range. The report lists Animal Owner, owner phone, animal ID, due date and the animal.
011 Admin Personnel/constructCertifiedLetter
Admin Personnel, using Microsoft Word and a saved template, constructs the certified letter indicating a deadline of 10 days from date of letter creation for compliance or a citation will be issued for their failure to comply.
