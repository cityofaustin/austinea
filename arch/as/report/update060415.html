Authorize Event Attendance
Use Case

Use Case Scope

The Citizen contacts animal services (via 311/customer service/direct call/email) to advertise their event and request Austin Animal Services personnel attend. An Animal Services person is assigned the request, and it is forwarded to the Animal Service Marketing team. The marketing team checks the calendar and determines if Austin Animal Services will be participating in the event. If so, the Animal services contact seeks volunteers for the event through the GivePulse application (if Volunteers are needed). Once volunteers are found (or if no volunteers are needed), the Animal Services contact reaches out to the Animal Services Marketing team to provide marketing materials, and then the Animal services contact scheduled the event on the calendar. The citizen is contacted and confirmation is given. If the marketing team determines the event is not something in which the city would like to invest time and money, or if no volunteers can be located and are needed, the Animal Services Contact reaches out to the Citizen to let them know Animal Services will not be attending the event.

Authorize Event Attendance Interface Descriptions

Type/InterfaceDescription
Citizen/
requestAnimalServiceResource		 The Citizen/Employee reaches out to Animal Services via email, phone, in person, etc, and requests they attend an event in the future.
Animal Services Contact/
determineAnimalServicesContact		 The Animal Services Contact fills out an EventsApprovalRequest, this form includes date, time, size of event, purpose, materials needed, costs, volunteer need if any, location, setup start/stop time, however the actual person to attend is not yet assigned. This form is routed to the Marketing team.
Marketing Team/
displayEventCalendar		 The Marketing team accesses the system and determines if the date requested is available for staff. If none are located, the Marketing team hangs by the side of the road with "pet work for a sandwich" billboards.
Marketing Team/
notifyAnimalServicesContactOfEvent		 The Marketing Team determines if the event should be attended, and communicates with the Animal Services Contact to convey the decision.
Animal Services Contact/
establishVolunteerDeadlineDate		 The Animal Services Contact determines the date by which volunteers must be acquired, who will attend the event and who the backup contact will be
GivePulse/
requestVolunteerForEvent		 The Animal Services Contact accesses GivePulse and advertises the need for volunteer(s).
Volunteer Coordinator/
provideVolunteerAssistance		 The Animal Services Contact reaches out to the Animal services Volunteer coordinate and requests assistance with finding volunteers for the event.
Animal Services Contact/
notifyCitizenNoEvent		 The Animal Services Contact reaches out to the citizen and indicates Austin Animal Services will not be attending the event.
Marketing Team/
provideMarketingMaterials		 The Animal Services contact goes to the marketing team and requests marketing materials (if there is a need.) This might include flyers, give-aways, posters, etc.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
addEventToCalendar		 The Animal Services Contact accesses the system and add the event to the calendar.
Animal Services Contact/
confirmEventWithCitizen		 The Animal Services Contact reaches out to the Citizen to confirm Austin Animal Services personnel will be attending the event.
Marketing Team/
evaluateEventReviewForm		 After the event, the Animal Services Contact will fill out the EventReviewForm detailing information about the event like: number of people attended, Outreach message, quantity of promo's, etc.
