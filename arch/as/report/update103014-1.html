Modify Fleet Driver Authorization
Use Case

Use Case Scope

Once a month Human resources receives a report from the Texas Department of Defense with City of Austin Employees, and their respective driving points. HR will distribute this report to fleet managers within each department.  The Executive Fleet Manager receives the report and sends it to the Fleet Manager. The Fleet Manager Accesses the system and restricts fleet usage for people the report indicates not allowed to use vehicles (based on department policy) and/or authorizes vehicle usage for employees whose points have expired.

Modify Fleet Driver Authorization Interface Descriptions

Type/InterfaceDescription
Human Resources/
deliverDOTResults		 HR develops and delivers a report indicating employees of a specific department and their authorization to use or not use a fleet vehicle, based on Department of Transportation Driver's Points.
Executive Fleet Manager/
processDOTResponse		 Human resources send the HR DOT monthly report to the Executive Fleet Manager.  The Executive Fleet Manager forwards the report to the Fleet Manager.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
updatePersonInfo		 The Fleet Manager Accesses the system and looks up and assign an employee to a specific fleet vehicle. The system ensures the “isAllowedToUseFleet” attribute of the PersonInfo is set to True or returns an error. Additionally the system insures the date of the last driver’s certification is within three years.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
notifyEmpoyeeOfFleetRestriction		 The system sends notification to an employee indicating inability to assign them to a specific vehicle on the fleet. The notification includes the reason for restriction and the timeframe for which the restriction exists.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
notifyFieldOfficerOfFleetUsageAuthorization		 The system sends the employee associated with the fleet vehicle reserved an email indicating the vehicle Id No, the date, time, and specifics of the vehicle they will use. This information includes make, model, color, whether animals can be transported, and the person to whom the reservation was made.   If no reservation has been made, the employee is notified they are eligible for vehicle reservation.
