Manage Foster Placement
Use Case

Use Case Scope

A Citizen contacts Austin Animal Services and is directed to fill out an application for Foster Care by the Application Provider. The Foster Coordinator processes the application and adds the Citizen to the system, establishing a Person ID for them. The Foster Coordinator clarifies any points of contention with the application, adds the Citizen to the Yahoo! Group, which in turn generates an invite to the group to the Citizen. The Foster Coordinator then sends a welcome email that instructs them to join a Yahoo! Group. Once the Citizen joins the Yahoo! Group, they are part of the Foster Care Providers.

Manage Foster Placement Interface Descriptions

Type/InterfaceDescription
Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/
generateFostercarePlacementReport		 The Foster Coordinator uses the system to generate a report of all animals that have been marked as needing foster care (young kittens/puppies, nursing mothers, animals recovering from illness or injury, and animals not faring well in shelter setting). The report lists all animals that need to go to foster care, and is not date centric.
Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/
lookupAnimalAndPersonInfo		 The Foster Coordinator reviews the animals listed on the report, and records salient information such as special needs, medical homecare needs, behavioral needs, etc.
YouTube/
createAndUploadVideoOfAnimal		 The Foster Coordinator takes video of the animals listed on the FosterCarePlacementReport, and uploads the video clips to YouTube. Occasionally, the videos are uploaded to Facebook as well.
Yahoo Website/
createAnimalPostingInYahooGroup		 The Foster Coordinator sends an email to the Yahoo! Group, which automatically posts the prepared descriptions/biographies of available animals (this will include photos, links to video, animal information, etc.).
Yahoo Website/
requestAnimalForFostercare		 The Foster Care Provider will reply to the Foster Care Coordinator voicing interest on a specific animal or set of animals.
Foster Coordinator/
determineFostercareProvider		 The Foster Coordinator evaluates the Foster Care Providers to determine the best fit for each animal.
Foster Coordinator/
respondToFosterQuestionsViaEmail		 If there are outstanding questions for the Foster Care Provider about animals for which they have voiced interest, the Foster Coordinator will send an email to the Foster Care Provider for clarification.
Provide Animal Rescue and Shelter/
addAndUpdatePersonAndAnimalInfo		 Customer Service/Foster Coordinator/Other users will access the system and add or update person and animal info. They will include information like owner, owner address, pet name, pet breed, pet sex, pet microchip info, rabies certificate information, as well as other important information. If the animal or person is already in the system, Customer Service uses the system to update the information. In the case of Trappers, add the date they signed the Standards of Conduct and the Standard Operating Procedure. In the event of Volunteer Applicant for Trappers, the Outreach Education Team will access the system and provide information from the Trapper Application to create a person info record for the Applicant (including verification date and time for the signing of the Standard of Conduct and the Standard Operating Procedure), if one does not already exist. If a person info record exists, the Outreach Education Team will update the information as necessary based on the Applicant's information. For Spay/Neuter, the user modifies the sex of the animal from M/F (male/female) to S/N (Spay/Neuter), and records the date of surgery, date processed, and vet clinic ID (the system maintains a list of all vet clinics on record within the area.) Foster Coordinators create person info records for Citizens acting/assisting with foster care, and annotate animal info records with information indicating Foster Care Providers who have taken ownership of the animals.
Foster Coordinator/
scheduleAndPickupAnimal		 The Foster Care Provider confirms desire to receive animal and schedules a pickup date and time with the Foster Coordinator, and then picks up the animal.
Foster Coordinator/
createAndEmailAnimalFosterCareSchedule		 The Foster Coordinator emails a foster pet schedule that includes due dates for needed medical care, and instructions on how to receive the medical care. The email includes an adoption packet, so the Foster Care Provider can begin marketing the animal from their home.
Foster Coordinator/
processAnimalAdoption		 The Foster Care Provider emails the Foster Coordinator indicating they have an adopter for the animal; so, it can now be removed from Austin Animal Center's website.
Foster Coordinator/
emailAdopteeAnimalFinalPaperwork		 The Foster Coordinator sends an email to the Adoptee outlining a schedule of required follow on items for the animal (when and how Spay/Neuter occurs, date and time of shots, etc. )
