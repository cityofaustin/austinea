Intake Animal
Use Case

Use Case Scope

Animals are received by Intake Staff or by Animal Protection. The Customer Contact verifies jurisdiction authority limiting services to the appropriate citizens (Austin City, and (unincorporated Travis County). The Customer Contact determines if the animal is a stray, or owner surrender, or public assist (temporary shelter). If the animal is accepted in to care, the Customer Contact enters information about the person surrendering the animal as well as the animal itself. The Customer Contact also collects the location where animal is found. Animal Geo location information is determined and fed to a website straymapper.com - An interface with FINDING ROVER is desirable. The system provides a means for the Animal Source to enter animal personality profile information into the system via the web or a kiosk and for Customer Contacts to access that information. The system provides the ability to integrate with a micro-chip scanner. The system provides a service Intake from the Austin Customer Service 311 system.

Intake Animal Interface Descriptions

Type/InterfaceDescription
Customer Contact/
provideAnimal		 The Animal Source provides an animal to the customer contact for intake. The Customer contact inquires to ensure the Animal is within a jurisdictional boundary. Jurisdictions covered are only (unincorporated) Travis county and city of Austin County. The Customer Contact also searches other resources to determine if people are in the jurisdiction, as well as checks the identification of the Animal Source.
Customer Contact/
scanAnimalForMicrochip		 The Customer Contact scans the animal for the presence of a microchip. The System automatically interfaces with the microchip scanner, locating matching animal info records when a match exists.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
locateAnimalSourceInDatabase		 The Customer Contact accesses the system and attempts to locate the Animal Source Person record as well as the Animal Info. The system provides the ability to search for Person Information and displays closely matched searches even if incorrectly enter in the search field. The system provides the ability to change info in the Person Identification record. The Customer Contact checks to see if the Animal Source has been to the Animal Shelter before (are they already on the animal info table). The System provides a search for any id that can locate a known owner such as: Drivers License, address, first name, last name, Date of Birth, animal name, phone number, email address, etc. The Customer Contact can query animal info based on characteristics like (size, breed, temperament, etc.) This search feature can also be used to try and find matches for potential adopters.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
updatePersonInfo		 The Customer Contact verifies the information in the Person Info Record is correct for the Animal Source, or gathers information associated with the Animal Source to create a Person Info record. The Person info record has fields like (name, address, phone, Date of birth, Sex, Companions Name, Drivers License Number and email address.) The System provides mechanisms to search for a Person Info record by drivers license, phone, animal info, email, name, and/or address.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
addPhotoToAnimalRecord		 If no photograph of the animal exists on the Animal Info Record, the Customer Contact uses the system to upload and attach a photograph of the animal to the Animal Info Record. The system provides an automated mechanism for taking and attaching photos to animal info records.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
intakeAnimal		 The Customer Contact accesses the system and attempts to locate the Animal info record associated with the Animal. If the Animal Info cannot be located, a new record is added for the animal. Animal Info includes breed, sex, (spay or Neutered), age, markings, Scars, injuries, and a unique Animal ID No (created by the system when a new record is added.)The Customer Contact annotates whether the animal is stored in a holding kennel, and will print a kennel card. The System provides a mechanism to search for Animal Info records by Owner, by Breed, memo into, and by address.The system provides a mechanism to merge to Animal Info Records, should duplicates be found. The System provides a mechanism to convert street addresses to Geo Location info used with Stray Mapper.
Animal Source/
provideOwnerSurrenderProfile		 The Customer Contact provides the Animal Source the Owner Surrender Profile declaring their intention of abandoning the animal with Animal Services. The Animal Sources fills out and signs the Owner Surrender profile and provides it to the customer contact.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
recordOwnerSurrenderInfo		 The Customer Contact accesses the system and enters the details associated with the Owner Surrender Profile into the Person Info Record, as well as on the Animal Info record.
Provide Animal Shelter and Rescue/
addMemo		 The Customer Contact uses the system to add memo's to the Animal Info record. A memo describes the animal's behavior, condition, temperament, etc. could be based on templates for routine cases. Memo's are added to attach critical information to an animal record, or to place the animal on "hold" (meaning action must be taken to remove the hold before the animal can be released.) The Customer Contact can query animal info based on characteristics like (size, breed, temperament, etc.) This can be used to try and find matches for potential adopters. Memos can be added for Microchip information, Handleability (Declawed cats, spade/neutered,etc) , Rabies Tag information, Socialization notes (kid friendly, lived with dogs/cats, etc.), Hold for (Animal protection officer (APO), Veterinary (DVM), Austin Police Department (APD), Travis County (TCSO), Quarantine, owner surrender, and Bite Reports). For commonly entered memo items, The System provides automatic, pre defined field population. The templates are customizable by and used by the staff in general through role based permissions.
