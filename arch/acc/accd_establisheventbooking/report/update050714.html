Manage Out of Service Inventory
Use Case

Use Case Scope

The Materials Planner uses the System to choose an Inventory Item that must be taken out of service. The System presents the Material Planner with a calendar of reservations for the Inventory Item. The Materials Planner evaluates reasons for reservations and the need for taking Inventory Item out of service. If reservation need outweighs the 'out of service' need, the Materials Planner chooses an available start date/time and end date/time during which the Inventory Item will be out of service. The Materials planner uses the system to specify the reason the Inventory Item is 'out of Service'. The Materials Planner completes the out of service reservation and during the date range specified, the System changes the Inventory Item's status to 'Out of Service'. If the 'out of service' need outweighs reservation needs, the Materials Planner chooses the start date/time and end date/time during which Inventory Item will be out of service as well as reason. The System changes the Inventory Item's status to 'Out of Service'. The System notifies effected Employees letting them know their reservation is no longer valid and includes the reason entered by the Materials Planner. The System presents the Employee with the option to make a future reservation. The Inventory Item remains 'out of service' until the end date/time or until the Materials Planner changes status back to 'In Service'. If Inventory Item service extends beyond end date/time the Materials Planner uses the System to extend the 'out of service' reservation. If reservations exist that conflict with 'out of service' periods the System notifies effected Employees letting them know their reservation is no longer valid and includes the reason entered by the Materials Planner. The System presents the Employee with the option to make a future reservation.
