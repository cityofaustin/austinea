Establish Forecast And Budget
Use Case

Use Case Scope

The Sales Manager and Internal Operations Experts use the system to enter Events as Forecast Items up to five Fiscal Years. Forecasts are established along Fiscal Year boundaries and can be modified at any time. Budgets are established along Fiscal Year boundaries and cannot be modified once approved by Chain of Command. The Budget and Forecast includes all estimates for an Event for each Revenue Category: Facility Gross Rental, Credit Food & Beverage, Room Night Credit, Facility Net, Damages, Equipment Rental, Technical Services, Utility Services, Telecom, General Labor, Security, Garage Daily, Garage Validation, Garage Contract, Garage Total, Catering, Audio Visual, Rental, Scooter Rental, Retail Sales, Shoe Shine, Other Concessions. Must have the ability to add new Revenue Categories. Note: The Budget is submitted to the City of Austin Budget Office in May and is approved by September.
