Provision Digital Signs Use Case Model

Scope: 

If a Site Tour is required, the Sales Manager asks the Digital Sign Administrator to create Kiosk Digital Signs for the site tour and includes a Site Tour Plan and Event Logo and the Digital Sign Administrator uses the system to store and schedule Kiosk Digital Signs. The Event Coordinator asks the Client to complete the Permission to Publish Form and return the form along with an Event Logo. The completed Permission to Publish is returned to the Contract Administrator who uses the Event Booking System to store the form and Event Logo. The Event Coordinator asks the Client to provide Digital Sign Content and the Client returns Digital Sign Content to the Digital Sign Administrator. If the Client requires Advanced Digital Signs, the Client includes an Event Space Schedule. If the Client requires Meeting Room Signs, the Client includes a Meeting Room Schedule. If answers are required of the Client, the Digital Sign Administrator asks the Client to answer Digital Sign Content Questions and this process repeats until all questions are answered. If additional artwork is required, the Digital Sign Administrator asks the Graphic Designer to produce Digital Sign Additional Artwork. If a special database configuration is required, the Digital Sign Administrator asks the Database Administrator to configure the database and provide confirmation. When all materials are received, the Digital Sign Administrator uses the system to store and schedule Basic Digital Signs. If the Client indicated on the Permission to Publish Form the event was a Public Event, the Digital Sign Administrator stores and schedules Marquee Sign Content. If the Client requested Advanced Digital Signs, the Digital Sign Administrator stores and schedules Digital Sign Content inclusive of the Event Space Schedule. If the Client requested Meeting Room Signs, the Digital Sign Administrator stores and schedules Digital Sign Content inclusive of the Meeting Room Schedule. With all digital signs provisioned, if Digital Sign Service Fees are required, the Digital Sign Administrator asks the Utility Services Coordinator to store these fees in the Event Booking System.

createKioskDigitalSignsForSiteTour
 The Sales Manager sends a SiteTourNotification to the Digital Sign Administrator, requesting they create Kiosk Digital Signs for a Site Tour. The SiteTourNotification contains a SiteTourPlan and an EventLogo.
FS002
 Digital Sign Software/
storeAndScheduleKioskDigitalSigns		 The Digital Sign Administrator uses the Digital Sign Software to store and schedule the Kiosk Digital Signs, using DigitalSignContent and DigitalSignStartEndDateTime.
FS003
 Client/
completePermissionToPublishForm

 The Event Coordinator asks the Client to complete the Permission to Publish Form and in return receives a completed Permission to Publish Form and EventLogo.
FS004
 Event Booking System/
storePermissionToPublishForm		 The Contract Administrator uses the Event Booking system to store the PermissionToPublishForm and the EventLogo.
FS005
 Client/
provideDigitalSignMaterials 		The Event Coordinator sends a DigitalSignMaterialsRequest to the Client and in return the Client sends DigitalSignContent to the Digital Sign Administrator.
FS006
 Digital Sign Administrator/
recordEventSpaceSchedule		 If the Client requires Advanced (Directional) Digital Signs around the interior of the facility, the Client asks the Digital Sign Administrator to record an EventSpaceSchedule.
FS007Digital Sign Administrator/
recordMeetingRoomSignSchedule		If the Client requires Meeting Room Signs, the Client asks the Digital Sign Administrator to record a MeetingRoomSchedule. The MeetingRoomSchedule is in the form of an Excel template provided to the Client via the Austin Convention Center or Palmer Event Center websites.
FS008Client/
answerDigitalSignContentQuestions		If answers are required of the Client, the Digital Sign Administrator asks the Client to answer DigitalSignContentQuestions and in return receives DigitalSignContentAnswers. This process loops until all answers are received.
FS009Graphic Designer/
createArtwork		If additional artwork is required for digital signs, the Digital Sign Administrator sends a DigitalSignAdditionalArtworkRequest to the Graphic Designer and in response receives DigitalSignAdditionalArtwork.
FS010Database Administrator/
configureDatabase		If a database configuration is required, the Digital Sign Administrator sends a DatabaseConfigurationRequest to the Database Administrator, resulting in a DatabaseConfigurationConfirmation from the Database Administrator.
FS011Digital Sign Software/
storeAndScheduleBasicDigitalSigns		The Database Administrator uses the Digital Sign Software to store and schedule Basic Digital Signs, using DigitalSignContent and DigitalSignStartEndDateTime. Basic Digital Signs simply contain the name and logo of an event.
FS012Digital Sign Software/
storeAndScheduleMarqueeSignContent		If the event is a public event (not private) the Digital Sign Administrator uses the Digital Sign Software to store and schedule the Marquee Digital Signs, using DigitalSignContent and DigitalSignStartEndDateTime.
FS013Digital Sign Software/
storeAndScheduleAdvancedDigitalSigns		If Advanced Digital Signs are required, the Digital Sign Administrator uses the Digital Sign Software to store and schedule Advanced Digital Signs using DigitalSignContent, EventSpaceSchedule and DigitalSignStartEndDateTime. Advanced Digital Signs are those that contain more specific directions and directional indicators, for example: Registration in Hall 1, Exhibits in Halls 2 & 3, etc. with associated arrows pointing to the event halls.
FS014Digital Sign Software/
storeAndScheduleMeetingRoomSigns		If Meeting Signs are required, the Digital Sign Administrator uses the Digital Sign Software to store and schedule Meeting Room Signs using DigitalSignContent, DigitalSignStartEndDateTime and the MeetingRoomSchedule.
FS015Utility Services Coordinator/
recordDigitalSignServicesFees		If any fees are required for Digital Sign Services, the Digital Sign Administrator notifies the Utility Services Coordinator with a request to record DigitalSignServiceFees.
FS016Event Booking/
storeDigitalSignServicesFees		The Utility Services Coordinator uses the Event Booking system to store the DigitalSignServiceFees for the specific event.
