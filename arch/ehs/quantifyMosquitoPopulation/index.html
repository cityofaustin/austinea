Use Case Specification
Quantify Mosquito Population

Scope: Based on seasonal activities, the Surveillance Technician identifies an area interest and identifies Gravid Trap (mosquito trap) placement. Occasionally, a complaint is received by citizens which may influence trap emplacement. The Surveillance Technician places traps and uses the system (mobile device) to enter its location (currently using GPS) locator. The traps are left overnight and picked up prior to 12 PM the next day to ensure live specimens. The Surveillance Technician brings the specimens to local state lab for species identification and testing (i.e., West Nile Virus). The senior/supervisor receives the test results via email from the state lab and enters the information into the system (currently an Access Database). The supervisor converts the data to a GIS layer for upload and display on public map. If observable standing water, the Surveillance Technician may use larvacide and use the system to record the amount displaced, pesticide used, location and wind speed for state licensing compliance purposes. The Surveillance Technician sprays in unincorporated and contracted incorporated areas.
