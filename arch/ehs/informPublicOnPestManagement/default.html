Use Case Specification
Inform Public on Pest Management

Scope: Triggered by citizen requesting a site visit for either stinging insects or rodents, the Surveillance Technician verifies ownership of site via TCAD and CC&B (by address). By appointment, the Surveillance Technician visits the site to perform area surveillance. Bait box may be placed for later retrieval. The Surveillance Technician may use rodenticide and record the amount displaced, rodenticide used, location and wind speed for state licensing compliance purpose. Record educational materials handed out such as pamphlets, etc. Free traps of various verity may be given out (also recorded). The system tracks quantity for reordering purposes.
