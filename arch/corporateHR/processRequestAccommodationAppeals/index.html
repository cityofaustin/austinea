Process Appeal of Reasonable Accommodation Request
Use Case Model

Scope: The Customer uses the Operating system to copy a file from their disk location into the designated FTP site. The operating system displays an acceptable use banner, and prompts the user to acknowledge and confirm their data is in accordance with City policy related to placing data on an FTP site. Once confirmed, the Operating system moves the file to the FTP location. Once the copy to the new location is complete, DLP services evaluate the file and determine if there is a pattern match indicating potential policy violation. If issues are perceived, the DLP software moves the offending file into a "Quarantine" area for further evaluation. The DLP software sends email to a distribution list of Security team members, as well as notifies the Customer of a potential problem. Security evaluates the quarantined file. If problems are confirmed, Security notifies the Chain of Command (of the Customer) of the issue, and determines next steps. If a false positive was reported, the Security Team moves the quarantined file into FTP site, and notifies the Customer of file acceptance.

Process Appeal of Reasonable Accommodation Request
Sequence Diagram

Req# Type/Interface - Requirement Description Performance
001 City Managers Office/acceptAppeal
Original Applicant or Agent of Applicant appeals the WrittenDecision to deny or grant an AlternativeReasonableAccommodation within 30 calendar days from the mailing of the WrittenDecision. The Applicant appeals in writing and includes the grounds for Appeal. The Applicant has the option to submit multiple Appeal(s). The AppealDecision is determined by the City Managers Office (CMO). If the AppealDecision is not issued in 30 calendar days from the date the Appeal is received, the RequestAccommodation is granted. The CMO mails the written AppealDecision to the ADA office and the Applicant.
002 City Managers Office/transcribeAppealRequest
If the Applicant needs assistance to submit an Appeal the Department Director Designee provides assistance transcribing a verbal request into a written Appeal.

003 City Managers Office/accommodateExpediteRequest
The Applicant has the option to request an expedited review of Appeal. The CMO office does the best they can to meet the expedited review.
004 Law Office/createConsultationMeeting
In the event of an Appeal, a formal review by CMO, Legal Department, and the American's with Disabilities (ADA) office is convened for final disposition prior to the 30 calendar day expiration. All interested parties must come to a unanimous decision.
005 Law Office/reviewRisksToCity
If the decision is a denial of Appeal, the City Manager’s Office and Legal Department review risks of denial resulting in either upholding the denial or approval of Appeal.
006 City Managers Office/createAppealDecision
The CMO creates an AppealDecision. The status is 'closed - with appeal'
007 City Managers Office/approveOriginalRequestAccommodation
The CMO approves original RequestAccommodation.
008 Accommodation Requestor/receiveAppealDecision
The CMO sends the AppealDecision to original Applicant.
009 Americans With Disablities Office/receiveAppealDecision
The CMO sends the AppealDecision to ADA offices.
