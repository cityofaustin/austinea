Review Feedback
Use Case Model

Scope: The Feedback Reviewer receives an assigned ServiceRequest311Feedback and reviews to determine the response required. If the ServiceRequest311Feedback was incorrectly assigned, the Feedback Reviewer starts the Create Feedback Request and Triage use case to submit Feedback concerning the 311 Command Center's assignment of the ServiceRequest. The Feedback Reviewer assigns the ServiceRequest311Feedback to the appropriate workgroup to resolve. If the Feedback is invalid, the Feedback Reviewer ends their review. If the Feedback is resolvable by a workgroup, the Feedback Reviewer assigns the ServiceRequest311Feedback to the appropriate workgroup to resolve.

001 Feedback Reviewer/Review Feedback Service Request and Determine Response Required
The Feedback Reviewer reviews the assigned ServiceRequest311Feedback and determines the appropriate response. If the ServiceRequest311Feedback is invalid the Feedback Reviewer proceeds to completing the ServiceRequest311Feedback. If the assigned Feedback Reviewer's workgroup completes the Feedback the Feedback Reviewer assigns the ServiceRequest311Feedback to the appropriate staff member. If the ServiceRequest311Feedback was incorrectly assigned the Feedback Reviewer initiates the Create Feedback Request and Triage use case. The Feedback Reviewer then assigns the ServiceRequest311Feedback to the appropriate staff member.

003 Feedback Reviewer/Assign Feedback to Appropriate Staff
The Feedback Reviewer assigns the ServiceRequest311Feedback to the appropriate workgroup/staff to resolve. Once assigned, the ServiceRequest311Feedback is routed to the appropriate staff's queue in the system.
