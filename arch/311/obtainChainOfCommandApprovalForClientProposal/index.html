Obtain Chain of Command Approval for Client Proposal
Use Case Model

Scope: The Client Management Team submits the ClientProposal, either draft or updated, to the Chain of Command. The Chain of Command reviews the ClientProposal and determines whether to proceed with the ClientProposal. The Chain of Command's review results in either: approval, final approval, deferral, denial, a request for more information, or additional resources assigned to the ClientProposal. If additional resources are assigned a 311 Work Group SPOC is assigned and performs Resarch and creates a FeasibilityStudy to supplement the ClientProposal. The 311 Work Group SPOC sends their Research and FeasibilityStudy to the Client Management Team. If revisions are required to the ClientProposal, or the ClientProposal is denied, deferred,approved by a lower level of the Chain of Command, or more information is required, the Client Management Team updates the ClientProposal as appropriate. If the ClientProposal is denied/deferred/approved the Client Management Team sends Notification to the Client Department.

Obtain Chain of Command Approval for Client Proposal
Activity Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Client Management Team/Submit Client Proposal to Chain of Command
The Client Management Team submits the draft, or updated, ClientProposal to the Chain of Command. If a ClientProject's Requirements modified the scope of the approved ClientProposal the Client Management Team updates the ClientProposal and submits for approval.
002 Chain of Command/Review Client Proposal and Determine Outcome
The Chain of Command reviews the ClientProposal. The Chain of Command review occurs at three levels, depending on complexity of the ClientProposal: Client Relations & Information Services Manager, Customer Service Managers, and Process Manager. Chain of Command review may involve the Client Management Team, other Resources, and Managers as needed. The Chain of Command review results in either: approval, request for modifications, denial, or deferral. If additional resources are required to gather information the Chain of Command sends notification to 311 Work Group SPOC(s) to assist with the ClientProposal.
003 311 Work Group SPOC/Perform Research and Create Feasibility Study
The 311 Work Group SPOC provides information and performs Research to assist updating the ClientProposal. The 311 Work Group SPOC completes a Feasibility Study for their specific area of expertise and sends the FeasibilityStudy along with their Research to the Client Management Team.
004 Client Management Team/Modify and Update Client Proposal,and Notify Client Department as Necessary
The Client Management Team modifies and updates the ClientProposal based on feedback from the Chain of Command and 311 Work Groups SPOC(s). If additional Research is performed, or a FeasibilityStudy is created, the information is added to the ClientProposal. If the ClientProposal is deferred or denied, the Client Management Team sends Notification to the Client Department indicating the decision by the Chain of Command. If the ClientProposal is approved, and additional Chain of Command approval is required, the ClientProposal is sent to the next level of the Chain of Command. If the Chain of Command provides final approval, the Client Management Team sends Notification to the Client Department of the approval.
