Request 311 Services for Client Department
Use Case Model

Scope: After Requirements have been gathered completely for the ClientProject, the Client Management Team requests the appropriate 311 services from 311 Work Groups: ServiceRequestTypeChange, WebsiteChange, ReferenceMaterialChange, etc. See Extension Points on Use Case Diagram for possible services that can be requested. The Client Management Team updates the ClientProject, Timeline, and sends Notification of the requested service completion to the ClientProject stakeholders. If the requested services are not finished the Client Management Team sends periodic notification until all services are completed. Once all services have been completed the ClientProject is ready for go-live.

Request 311 Services for Client Department
Activity Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Client Management Team/Request Services from 311 Work Groups
The Client Management Team requests 311 services from 311 Work Groups to complete the necessary changes for the Client Department. Some services requested include ServiceRequestType change, Website update, Reference Material (KnowledgeBaseArticle, FAQ) update, etc. (See use case diagram for list of potential use cases to invoke).
002 Client Management Team/Update Client Project, Timeline, and Notify Stakeholders of Progress
The Client Management Team updates the ClientProject, Timeline, and sends Notification to the ClientProject stakeholders on the requested services' progression. If the requested services are not complete, the Client Management Team continues to monitor progress of services and sends periodic Notification until all services are completed. Once all services have been completed the ClientProject is ready to continue to the go-live phase.
