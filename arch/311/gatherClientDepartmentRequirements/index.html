Gather Client Department Requirements
Use Case Model

Scope: The Client Management Team creates the ClientProject from the approved ClientProposal. The ClientProject includes a Timeline to track progress and estimated dates for deliverables. The Client Management Team meets with the Client Department to gather Requirements to fulfill the ClienProject. The Client Management Team invites any necessary resources to the Client Department meeting to complete Requirements gathering. If after the Client Department meeting additional consulting services are required by 311 Work Group SPOC(s) the Client Management Team requests the appropriate 311 Work Group SPOC(s) provide consulting services. The 311 Work Group SPOC(s) assist in gathering additional Requirements. The Client Management Team updates the ClientProject with all gathered Requirements, updates the Timeline, and sends Notification of project status to stakeholders. The Client Management Team then determines if sufficient Requirements have been gathered or if the Requirements gathered modify the scope of the ClientProject. If additional Requirements are required the Client Management Team meets with the Client Department and repeats the Requirements gathering process. If the Requirements gathered modify the scope of the ClientProject, Chain of Command approval is required to proceed. If all Requirements have been gathered, and the ClientProject scope isn't changed the ClientProject is ready to proceed to requesting services from 311 Work Groups.

Gather Client Department Requirements
Activity Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Client Management Team/Create Client Project
The Client Management Team creates a ClientProject based on the ClientProposal. The ClientProject contains a Timeline.
002 Client Management Team/Meet With Client Department and Gather Requirements
The Client Management Team meets with the Client Department to identify Requirements for the ClientProject. The Client Management Team invites any necessary 311 Work Group SPOC(s) to the meeting. Based on the meeting the Client Management Team adds Requirements to the ClientProject. If the Client Management Team requires additional assistance from a 311 Work Group SPOC to complete the Requirements then the Client Management Team contacts the appropriate 311 Work Group SPCOC(s) to provide consultation services.
003 311 Work Group SPOC/Provide Consulting Services to Gather Requirements
The 311 Work Group SPOC(s) identifies additional Requirements. If additional meetings with the Client Department are required the 311 Work Group SPOC(s) notifies the Client Management Team.
004 Client Management Team/Update Client Requirements Docs, Timeline, and Notify Staff of Progress
The Client Management Team updates the ClientProject, Timeline, and sends Notification (status on project and whether go-live date is impacted, project concerns) to the Chain of Command, and other project stakeholders/SPOCs (as necessary). Updates are sent typically on a bi-weekly (once every two weeks) schedule. The Client Management Team determines if additional Requirements gathering is necessary, if the Requirements gathered modify the approved ClientProposal, or if sufficient Requirements have been gathered to request services from 311 Work Groups.
