Provide Emergency Event Support
Use Case Model

Scope: The Chain of Command receives an EmergencyEvent activation Notification (either Incident Command or Emergency Operation Center).  The Chain of Command activates the EmergencyEvent staff by sending Notification to the activated staff. A Business Process Specialist receives the activation Notification.  The Business Process Specialist identifies staff for the EmergencyEvent using the EOCStaffSchedule.  The Business Process Specialist identifies a Business Process Specialist (acts as 311 liaison, social media manager, and public information officer) and Research Analyst (provides research and gathers information for CollectiveSummaryReport), estimates the EmergencyEvent duration, and develops Schedules (8 or 10 hour shifts).  The Business Process Specialist then sends Notification to the activated staff of the EmergencyEvent (type, name, location, duration, Schedule, etc.) and 311 Command Center. The identified staff attends the EmergencyEvent.  The Research Analyst gathers information and updates the CollectiveSummaryReport continuously throughout the EmergencyEvent.  The Business Process Specialist acts as the Austin 311 liaison and assists with managing information to/from Austin 311, coordinating changes to ServiceRequests, and attends meetings. The Chain of Command communicates updates to the Business Process Specialist during the EmergencyEvent. After the EmergencyEvent is concluded the Business Process Specialist sends Notification of the deactivation to Austin 311.  The Business Process Specialist then coordinates a lessons learned meeting.  Feedback gathered from the lessons learned meeting triggers the Create Feedback Request and Triage use case, as necessary.  The lessons learned meeting also reviews the CollectiveSummaryReport and updates, as necessary.  After the lessons learned meeting the Business Process Specialist sends the CollectiveSummaryReport to the Chain of Command for review, and then Homeland Security and Emergency Management.  The Business Process Specialist saves the CollectiveSummaryReport, and updates the EOCStaffSchedule for future events.

Provide Emergency Event Support
Activity Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Chain of Command/Activate Event Staff and Send Notification
The Chain of Command receives an emergency activation Notification. The Chain of Command sends an activation Notification to the EmergencyEvent's staff.
002 Business Process Specialist/Gather Event Information and Send Notification to Activated Staff
The Business Process Specialist receives the activation Notification, identifies staff for the EmergencyEvent using the EOCStaffSchedule to identify the appropriate staff. The Business Process Specialist identifies a Business Process Specialist (acts as 311 liaison, social media manager, and public information officer) and Research Analyst (provides research and gathers information for CollectiveSummaryReport), estimates the EmergencyEvent duration, and develops Schedules (8 or 10 hour shifts).  The Business Process Specialist then sends Notification to the activated staff of the EmergencyEvent (type, name, location, duration, Schedule, etc.) and 311 Command Center. 
003 Chain of Command/Send Operations Updates to Business Process Specialist
The Chain of Command sends update Notifications to the Business Process Specialist concerning the operation of the 311 Call Center.
004 Business Process Specialist/Provide Incident Command Support and Communicate Updates Between Austin 311 and Incident Command
The Business Process Specialist acts as the Austin 311 liaison and assists with managing information to/from Austin 311, coordinating changes to ServiceRequests, and attends meetings. The Business Process Specialist sends Notifications to 311 staff and receives Notification requesting information/updates. Business Process Specialists rotate work schedules to staff the EmergencyEvent continuously.
005 Research Analyst/Gather Incident Command Information and Update Summary Report
The Research Analyst gathers information and updates the CollectiveSummaryReport continuously throughout the EmergencyEvent using a CollectiveSummaryReportTemplate to provide a framework for the information gathered. Research Analysts rotate work schedules to staff the EmergencyEvent continuously.
006 Business Process Specialist/Send Incident Command Deactivation Notice
The Business Process Specialist receives event information deactivating the EmergencyEvent. The Business Process Specialist sends a deactivation Notification to Austin 311 staff and the EmergencyEvent is completed.
007 Business Process Specialist/Conduct Lessons Learned Meeting and Send Summary Report to Chain of Command & HSEM
The Business Process conducts a lessons learned meeting. The CollectiveSummaryReport is reviewed and updated. If Feedback is received that is actionable the Create Feedback Request and Triage use case is invoked. At the conclusion of the lessons learned meeting the Business Process Specialist sends the final CollectiveSummaryReport to Chain of Command and Homeland Security and Emergency Management (HSEM).
