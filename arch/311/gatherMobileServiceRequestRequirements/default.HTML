Gather Mobile Service Request Requirements
Use Case Model

Scope: During requirements gathering the Mobile App Lead coordinates a meeting with the internal mobile application team (Mobile App Lead, Business Process Specialists, Chain of Command, CSR team) to identify the Requirements necessary to complete the MobileAppDefinition for the Vendor. The Business Process Specialist then meets with the Client Department to gather Requirements. The Business Process Specialist invites any support staff (Mobile App Lead, CSR manager, etc.) to the meeting to assist in Requirements gathering. The Business Process sends the Requirements to the Mobile App Lead who updates the MobileAppDefinitions with the Requirements, updates the MobileAppChangeRequest with progress, determines completeness of Requirements, and then notifies the internal team of progress. If additional Requirements are required another internal meeting is scheduled by the Mobile App Lead to identify Requirements necessary, and the Business Process Specialist meets with the Client Department again. This loop continues until the Requirements are satisfied. If the Requirements gathered modify the approved scope of the MobileAppChangeRequest the Requirements gathering phase ends and the MobileAppChangeRequest must be approved by the Chain of Command again as part of the review phase. The MobileAppChangeRequst is moved to a review pending status to indicate the needed approval. If the MobileAppDefinitions are complete the Mobile App Lead collects all MobileAppDefinition information for each ServiceRequestType and sends to the Vendor. All MobileAppDefinitions are sent at once to the Vendor. The update process waits until all Requirements are gathered before proceeding. When the Vendor receives the MobileAppDefinition the Vendor reviews the document and determines whether to reject or accept the MobileAppDefinitions. If rejected, the Vendor sends an amended MobileAppDefinition to the Mobile App Lead, and the Requirements gathering phase loops until the gathered Requirements satisfy the amended MobileAppDefinition. If accepted, the Vendor sends a MobileAppAcceptanceCertificate to the Chain of Command. The Chain of Command signs the MobileAppAcceptanceCertificate and returns to the Vendor. When the signed MobileAppAcceptanceCertificate is received by the Vendor the requirements gathering phase is completed for the mobile app update cycle.

Gather Mobile Service Request Requirements
Activity Diagram

Req#Type/Interface - Requirement DescriptionPerformance
001 Mobile App Lead/Coordinate Meeting to Review Approved Changes, Mobile App Definition, and Identify Requirements to Gather
60 days before the last negotiated MobileAppGoLiveDate, the Mobile App Lead collects the approved MobileAppChangeRequests from the Chain of Command for the next round of updates. The Mobile App Lead coordinates a meeting with the internal team (Business Process Specialist, CSR Manager, Chain of Command) to review the MobileAppChangeRequest, MobileAppDefinition, and identify information to gather from the Client Department.
002 Business Process Specialist/Meet with Client Department and Gather Requirements
The Business Process Specialist coordinates a meeting with the Client Department and the internal team (CSR Configuration Manager, project team, etc.) to gather Requirements to complete the MobileAppChangeRequest, as needed. Requirements to gather include: FlexNotes/FlexNoteAnswers (including default answers) for Mobile App, TagLine, TargetDate, MobileSRName, Category, SPOTDescription, SPOTSLA, SRProcessFlow.
003 Mobile App Lead/Update Mobile Application Definition Document, Change Request, Determine Requirements Completeness, and Notify Internal Team
The Mobile App Lead uses the Requirements gathered from the Client to update the MobileAppDefinition and MobileAppChangeRequest. The Project Lead determines if the Requirements gathered are sufficient to complete the MobileAppDefinitionDocument for the specified change. If the Requirements gathered modify the scope of the approved MobileAppChangeRequest the MobileAppChangeRequest enters a review pending state and requires approval from the Chain of Command again. If the Requirements are incomplete additional Requirements gathering commences. If the Requirements are complete the Mobile App Lead prepares the MobileAppDefinition for submission to the Vendor. The Mobile App Lead sends notification to Stakeholders on the progress and status of the MobileAppChangeRequest.
004 Mobile App Lead/Send Mobile Application Definition Document to Vendor
The Mobile App Lead verifies the MobileAppDefinition and submits the completed MobileAppDefinition to the Vendor (often through an online upload). MobileAppDefinition is only uploaded once and is uploaded for all MobileAppChangeRequests.
005 Vendor/Review Mobile Application Document, Determine Response, and Notify Austin 311
The Vendor reviews the MobileAppDefinitionsDocument and determines whether to accept or reject the submitted information. The Vendor submits an AcceptanceCertificate to the Project Manager and Project Lead. The Vendor updates the MobileAppDefinitionsDocument with changes to conform to Vendor requirements and sends to the Project Manager and Project Lead.
006 Chain of Command/Sign Acceptance Certificate and Return to Vendor
The Chain of Command signs the MobileAppAcceptanceCertificate and returns to the Vendor. Signature of the MobileAppAcceptanceCertificate identifies the completion of the first phase of the Mobile App Update Process.
