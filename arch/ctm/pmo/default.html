«Capability» Manage Projects Initiates, plans, executes, controls, and closes the work of a team (could include waterfall, hybrid or agile methods) to achieve business specific goals and meet specified success criteria. A temporary endeavor designed to produce a unique product, service or result with a defined beginning and end (usually time-constrained, and often constrained by funding or deliverables) undertaken to meet unique goals and objectives to bring about beneficial change or added value.
«Capability» Coordinate Acquisition Coordinates scope and resources to deliver request for council action (RSA), request for proposal (RFP), request for quote (RFQ), request for input (RFI), contracts, budgets, etc. in order to acquire good and services on behalf of business operations. Coordinates vendor performance and feedback to ensure effective integrated solutions and desired business results. Ensures acquisitions meet local, state and federal compliance and guidelines. Provides plans, status, state (concerns and issues) and required actions to accomplish vendor delivery. Manages life-cycle risk from initiation to termination.
