IT Business Continuity Service Model

ServiceDescription
Provide IT Business Continuity Ensures critical business functions operate despite serious incidents or disasters, or able to recover to an operational state within a reasonably short period. As such, business continuity includes three key elements to include resilience, recovery and contingency. Risk is a core consideration since business continuity is primarily concerned with those business functions, operations, supplies, systems, relationships etc. critically important to achieve the operational objectives. Performs business impact analysis and risk management for the process of determining the relative importance or criticality of those elements, and in turn drives the priorities, planning, preparations and other business continuity management activities. Develops standards, policies, guidelines, and procedures needed to ensure services without stoppage, irrespective of the adverse circumstances or events.
