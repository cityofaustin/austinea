Support Services Group
Capability Area Architecture

The following graphical view depicts the CTM Support Service Group's environment and important stakeholders. The description captures deliverables (i.e., information elements) important to the stakeholders also considered important outcomes representing value. This viewpoint is an important step to identify services (capabilities) when stakeholders receive similar value. Service descriptions don't typically describe stakeholders and their role - this occurs later in the architecture development process. This viewpoint is not architecturally significant; however, it's useful as a brainstorming tool to maintain group focus on the services rendered in the form of important information elements as objects. The architect uses this information to identify patterns useful in service description.

Service Model
ServiceDescription
Support Procurement of IT Goods and Services Assists in the procurement of IT related goods and services by creating Request for Council Action (RCA), approving Requisition for Service (RQS) and Requisition for Master Agreement, and modifications to Delivery/Purchase Orders - assist in purchase requests, DIR master contract information, and citywide IT contracts. Provides vendor registration, invoices, payment, and unclaimed property checks. Maintains department financial records and billing.
Develop and Manage Department Budget Develops and manages department budget, forecast and Capital Improvement Plan (CIP) to include short-term and long-term. Responds to significant item questionnaire, financial audit requests, develops financial reports, cost allocation (rate definition) and inter-local budget.
Perform IT Organizational Support Performs administrative and technical support such as coordinating IT governance, reports to executive management, department business plan, CTM organizational charts, standard operating procedures, travel planning assistance and reimbursement, financial training and consultation, cash handling, etc. Assists in performance measures, strategic planning, Enterprise Architecture reporting, SharePoint site development and maintenance, and CTM newsletter. Manages records administration, ProCard, CTM fleet, fixed asset document, physical inventory, and coordinate responses to Public Information Requests, Council Item Update Reports, and Citizen Action Forms.
