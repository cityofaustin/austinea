CTM Wireless Service Model

ServiceDescription
Manage Radio Network Manage radio network to include voice radio communications and supporting infrastructure used by public safety, other public service agencies (non-enterprise general fund), and some enterprise departments such as utilities. Provide geographic coverage for Travis County and City of Austin. Provide master site control and services for regional and remote radio system partners, which requires limited support from other network services.
Provide Broadband Network Access Using commercial wireless carrier connections such as Long-Term Evolution (LTE), provide broadband access to public safety and public service customers - frequently used to access the City network using various controlled access privileges.
Provide WiFi Network Access Provide reliable WiFi network access to include Internet and City network - includes public and private WiFi access points.
Provide Voice Recording Provide voice recording services for Public Safety, general fund public service agencies and some enterprise departments.
Equip Public Service and Safety Vehicles Install vehicle electronic equipment and hardware (ex., two way radios, mobile data computers, digital video system, emergency lighting, weapons security systems, etc.). Support vehicle electronic equipment and hand-held devices such as radio, electronic citation writers and printers, radar guns and lasers.
Maintain Flood and Early Warning System Data Network Maintain licensed radio wireless data network for the City owned Flood Early Warning System.
