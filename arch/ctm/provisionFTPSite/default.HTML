Provision FTP Site
Use Case Model

Scope: The Customer accesses an online form and fills out a request for provisioning an FTP site. This form includes supervisor information, disk space needed, an acceptable use policy review, and an expiration date for when access to the FTP site will cease. The HelpDesk receives this information and determines if supervisory approval is needed. If it is, the HelpDesk contacts the supervisor and receives a go/no go decision. (NOTE: Supervisor approval is pursuant to current operational policies.) Once approved, the HelpDesk uses the FTP software to configure the FTP account - this includes establishing anonymous or password protected access to the site. Should the disk needs for the FTP site exceed the standard allocation (quota), the HelpDesk will engage the Operations team to provision the additional disk space needs. Upon completion, they HelpDesk contacts the Customer to convey completion and any password information.

Req# Type/Interface - Requirement Description Performance
001 HelpDesk/receiveRequestForFTPSite
The Customer accesses an online form and fills out information required to requisition the FTP site. The form includes the following information: Customer name, email, and phone, reason FTP site is needed, whether the FTP site will need to be secure, whether the data being stored is encrypted, the size of the data, how long the FTP site will be needed (when it expires), the customer's Supervisor name, Supervisor email, Supervisor phone number, the application providing the data, and the application receiving the data (if there is one). Additionally, this form includes an acceptable use policy review, and the online form includes a mechanism to indicate the Customer understands the acceptable use and agrees (by checking a box).
002 Chain of Command/approveFTPRequest
The HelpDesk contacts the supervisor provided on the FTPRequestForm to gain approval for creating the FTP site.

003 HelpDesk/closeHelpDeskTicket
The HelDesk accesses the Ticket associated with the FTP request and annotates the final disposition of the ticket.
004 Chain of Command/approveFTPRequest
The HelpDesk contacts the supervisor provided on the FTPRequestForm to gain approval for creating the FTP site.
005 FTP Server/createFTPAccount
The HelpDesk uses the FTP software to provision the new FTP account.
006 HelpDesk/requestExtendedDiskStorage
The HelpDesk contacts the operations team to requisition the necessary disk space for the FTP Site. Note, this only occurs when the needed space for the FTP site exceeds the standard "quota" established by operations.
007 Operations/provideDiskSpace
The Operations team allocates disk space for the FTPsite in accordance with the provided FTPRequestForm.
008 Operations/communicateDiskSpaceAllocation
The Operations team contacts the HelpDesk to notify them of the completion of the FTP Disk Space allocation.
009 HelpDesk/communicateFTPDetailsToCustomer
The HelpDesk Contacts the customer and communicates the completion of the FTP site creation. This is either by email, phone or ticket closure notification.
