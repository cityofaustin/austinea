Rob Byrd Presenting
at Innovate 2014

I'm happy to report that my presentation at IBM Innovate went really well. Oh my gosh, they gave me a whole hour! Dr Murray Cantor, IBM Distinguished Engineer, asked us to collaborate with him and his research team to establish measurable metrics (objectives) on important, key capabilities. Apparently, they've been been pondering this idea for some time but never had any architecture artifacts to entertain the dialog. Their concept is to identify architecture value that business leaders understand. This has potential to turn into something interesting.

I met with the Ration Software Architect (our architecture tool) product manager - he gave me his contact information and asked for product improvement suggestions. I learned something interesting. There's a company called CloudOne that hosts a SaaS solution for Maximo. The nature of SaaS inherently provides mobile solutions. However, everyone here agrees that we need to consolidate our active directories before enterprise SaaS solutions become viable.

I learned that Marc Ott wants us to brief the CMO on EA - hallelujah - it's about time!
