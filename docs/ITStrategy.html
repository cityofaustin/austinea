IT Principles

  • Use common enterprise-wide system and share available information systems
  • Share highly specialized IT resource across the City
  • Retire legacy assets based on pre-identified technology lifecycles, shared services opportunities, and cost/benefit analysis
  • Re-use existing capabilities before we buy; buy before we build
  • Purchase with full and open competition
  • Integrate IT strategic planning across the City to help focus on common and shared service opportunities
  • Build an agile organization that responds quickly and cost-effectively to City needs
  • Build appropriate redundancy and business continuity into critical systems
  • Secure every system to prevent unauthorized access
