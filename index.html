Our Mission

Enterprise Architecture (EA) is the process of translating business vision and strategy into effective enterprise change by creating, communicating, and improving the key requirements, principles, and models that describe the enterprise's future state and enable its evolution and transformation. This transformation process entails the analysis and design of an enterprise in its current and future states from a strategic, organizational, and technological perspective. The goals of EA are to improve the organizational efficiency, effectiveness, and agility by delivering business-aligned enterprise systems. Refer to Wikipedia definition of enterprise architecture for more explanation. Please watch the YouTube video Why Enterprise Architecture for a better understanding.

This site is dedicated to training, collaboration, and dissemination of City of Austin Enterprise Architecture artifacts.
